Universities Confronting the Legacy of Slavery

Please join us as the President’s Commission on Slavery and the University hosts the symposium Universities Confronting the Legacy of Slavery on October 16th–17th, 2014 in Charlottesville, Virginia.

Registration is free. Complete our registration form to attend.

Schedule

Thursday, October 16, 2014

Notes

Limited free parking (first come, first served) will be available at the Jefferson School City Center parking lot and the Amtrak parking lot near First Baptist Church. Ample parking is available near the University Cemetery in the parking lot off of Leake Drive. Shuttle bus transportation will be provided from First Baptist Church to the University Cemetery starting at 6:30pm. At 8:30 p.m., following the conclusion of the gravesite commemoration, bus transportation will be provided to return participants to First Baptist Church.

Refreshments will be provided at the Welcome Reception & Film Screening at the Jefferson School African American Heritage Center from 4:00-5:15 p.m.

The terrain by the University Cemetery is uneven in some areas and, in the event of recent rainfall, may be muddy. It is recommended that you wear comfortable walking shoes.
